About iBridge

Founded in 2015, iBridge Contact Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a leader in Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and Contact Centre Management. Headquartered in Roodepoort, Gauteng, South Africa, we specialize in delivering innovative solutions that bridge the gap between legacy systems and modern work environments.

Our mission is to provide tailored solutions that drive efficiency, reduce costs, and deliver measurable ROI for our clients. With a "Work from Anywhere" model powered by our ONE DIGITAL WORKPLACE platform, we ensure seamless operations and exceptional customer experiences.

Our expertise spans a wide range of services, including Digital Transformation Consulting, Cloud Services, AI & Machine Learning, and Omni-Channel Communication. At iBridge, we LISTEN, we UNDERSTAND, and we DELIVER.
