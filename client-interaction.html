Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our interaction channels

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. For after-hours support, please use our chatbot or leave a message, and we'll get back to you the next business day.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours. Complex inquiries may take longer, but we'll keep you updated on the progress.

Can I schedule a video consultation?

Yes, you can schedule a video consultation with our team. Please use the contact form on our website or call us to arrange a time that works for you.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes, our AI-powered chatbot is available 24/7 to assist you with basic inquiries and information about our services.
