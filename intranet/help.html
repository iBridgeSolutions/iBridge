Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  2. Enter your company email address
  3. Follow the instructions sent to your email
  4. If you don't receive an email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder
  5. For persistent issues, contact IT Support at itsupport@ibridge.co.za
How do I submit a leave request?

To submit a leave request:

  1. Navigate to the "HR" section in the main navigation
  2. Click on "Leave Management"
  3. Choose "Request New Leave"
  4. Fill in the required details (dates, type of leave, reason)
  5. Submit the request
  6. You will receive email notifications about the status of your leave request
Where can I find company policies?

Company policies are available in the Resources section:

  1. Go to the "Resources" page from the main navigation
  2. Click on the "Policies & Procedures" category
  3. Browse or search for the specific policy you need
  4. All policies are available in PDF format and can be downloaded
How do I report an IT issue?

To report an IT issue:

  1. Navigate to the "IT Support" section in the main navigation
  2. Click on "Submit a Ticket"
  3. Fill in the issue details, including priority level and a description
  4. Add any relevant screenshots or attachments
  5. Submit the ticket
  6. You will receive a ticket number for tracking purposes
  7. For urgent issues, you can also call the IT Help Desk at ext. 1234
How do I post an announcement on the newsboard?

Posting announcements is limited to admin users:

  1. If you have admin privileges, navigate to the Newsboard
  2. Click on the "+ New Announcement" button
  3. Fill in the required fields (title, content, category, etc.)
  4. Add any images or attachments
  5. Choose whether to send email notifications
  6. Publish the announcement
  7. If you don't have admin privileges but need to post an announcement, please contact your department manager or the communications team
How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information:

  1. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner
  2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click on the "Edit Profile" button
  4. Update your information as needed
  5. Click "Save Changes"
  6. Note that some information may need HR approval or can only be changed by HR (e.g., job title, department)
